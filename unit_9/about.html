Lyle Lorraine


About

Lyle Lorraine is an Oakland, CA, band comprised of Bay Area Natives Tyler Santoro, Matt Chris, Shaun Broda, & Pennsylvanian Mal Wood. Lyle Lorraine's sound is best described as a blend of Surf Rock, Garage Pop, and West Coast Kitsch.

Tyler Santoro
Vocals and rhythm guitar

Matt Chris
Lead guitar

Mal Wood
Drums and vocal harmony

Shaun Broda
Bass guitar
