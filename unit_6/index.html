About

Lyle Lorraine is an Oakland, CA band. Lyle's sound is a blend of Surf Rock, Garage Pop, and West Coast Kitsch.


Lyle Lorraine is comprised of Bay Area Natives Tyler Santoro (rhythm guitar), Matt Chris (lead guitar), Shaun Broda (base guitar), & hailing from ol' Pennsylvania Mal Wood (drums).
